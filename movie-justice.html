Justice League

Director/s: Zack Snyder, Joss Whedon

Starring: Ben Affleck, Henry Cavill, Amy Adams, Gal Gadot, Ezra Miller

Age Restriction: PG-13

Synopsis

Justice League is a superhero film based on the DC Comics superhero team of the same name.

The story follows Batman, played by Ben Affleck, as he teams up with Wonder Woman, played by Gal Gadot, to recruit a team of metahumans to face an otherworldly threat.

Together with Aquaman, The Flash, and Cyborg, they form the Justice League to battle the powerful villain Steppenwolf and his army of Parademons.

Filled with action-packed sequences, epic battles, and the formation of an iconic superhero team, "Justice League" is a thrilling adventure in the DC Extended Universe.
