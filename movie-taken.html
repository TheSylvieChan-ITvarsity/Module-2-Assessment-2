Taken 3

Director/s: Olivier Megaton

Starring: Liam Neeson, Maggie Grace, Forest Whitaker

Age Restriction: PG-13

Synopsis

Taken 3 follows Bryan Mills, played by Liam Neeson, as he is framed for the murder of his ex-wife, Lenore, played by Famke Janssen.

With the police in pursuit, Mills goes on the run to clear his name and protect his daughter, Kim, played by Maggie Grace.

As Mills evades the authorities and hunts down the true killers, he must use his particular set of skills to uncover a conspiracy that goes deeper than he could have imagined.

With high-octane action sequences and intense suspense, "Taken 3" is a gripping conclusion to the Taken film series, showcasing Bryan Mills' relentless determination to protect his family at all costs.
