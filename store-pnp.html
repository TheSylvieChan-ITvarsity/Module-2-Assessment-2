Welcome to Pick 'n Pay, your trusted neighborhood supermarket dedicated to bringing you quality products, exceptional value, and convenience.

For over 50 years, Pick 'n Pay has been a cornerstone of South African communities, providing a wide range of groceries, fresh produce, household essentials, and more.

Our commitment to sustainability and supporting local suppliers means you can shop with confidence, knowing that every purchase makes a difference.

Pick 'n Pay is here to make your shopping experience simple and rewarding. Visit us today and discover why we're the heart of your community.
