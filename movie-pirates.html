Pirates of the Caribbean:
Dead Man's Chest

Director/s: Gore Verbinski

Starring: Johnny Depp, Orlando Bloom, Keira Knightley

Age Restriction: PG-13

Synopsis

Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest is a fantasy swashbuckler film and the second installment in the "Pirates of the Caribbean" film series.

The story follows Captain Jack Sparrow, played by Johnny Depp, as he finds himself indebted to the legendary Davy Jones, captain of the Flying Dutchman, after a deal gone wrong.

Jack must now find a way to escape his impending fate of servitude aboard the cursed ship.

Meanwhile, Will Turner, played by Orlando Bloom, and Elizabeth Swann, played by Keira Knightley, are on a quest to save Jack and retrieve a mysterious key that holds the power to unlock a chest containing Davy Jones' heart.

As they face dangerous foes, supernatural forces, and the ever-elusive Jack Sparrow, their adventures lead them to the heart of the Caribbean.
