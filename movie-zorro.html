The Legend of Zorro

Director/s: Martin Campbell

Starring: Antonio Banderas and Catherine Zeta-Jones

Age Restriction: PG

Synopsis

The Legend of Zorro" is a 2005 swashbuckling adventure film directed by Martin Campbell. The story follows the legendary masked hero, Zorro, also known as Don Alejandro de la Vega, as he balances his life as a family man with his secret identity.

Set in 1850s California, Zorro must face new challenges as he battles corruption and fights for justice while trying to protect his wife, Elena, and their young son, Joaquin.

With thrilling action sequences, daring escapades, and a dash of romance, "The Legend of Zorro" is an exciting continuation of the Zorro saga.
