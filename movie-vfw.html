VFW

Director/s: Joe Begos

Starring: Stephen Lang, William Sadler, Sierra McCormick

Age Restriction: R

Synopsis

VFW is a action-horror film directed by Joe Begos. The story is set in a near-future dystopian world where a group of war veterans gathers at their local VFW (Veterans of Foreign Wars) bar.

Their evening takes a violent turn when a young woman named Lizard, played by Sierra McCormick, seeks refuge from a gang of drug-addled mutants.

The veterans, led by Fred, played by Stephen Lang, must defend their turf and protect Lizard at all costs. Armed with their military skills and an array of weapons, they engage in a fierce battle against the relentless attackers.

VFW is a gritty and intense action-horror film that pays homage to classic grindhouse cinema, featuring a cast of tough-as-nails characters fighting for survival against overwhelming odds.
