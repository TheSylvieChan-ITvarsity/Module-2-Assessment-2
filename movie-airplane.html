Airplane!

Director/s: Jim Abrahams, David Zucker, Jerry Zucker

Starring: Robert Hays, Julie Hagerty and features Leslie Nielsen

Age Restriction: PG

Synopsis

Airplane! is a comedy film parodying disaster movies.

The story follows Ted Striker, an ex-fighter pilot with a fear of flying, who boards a commercial airliner to win back his girlfriend, Elaine.

However, chaos ensues mid-flight, leading to a series of hilarious mishaps and absurd situations.

With rapid-fire jokes, visual gags, and memorable one-liners delivered by Leslie Nielsen as Dr. Rumack, Airplane! is a classic comedy known for its deadpan humor and enduring popularity.
