Ocean Basket

Welcome to Ocean Basket, where the taste of the sea meets warm hospitality in every dish. For over two decades, Ocean Basket has been South Africa's favorite seafood destination, serving up an array of fresh, delicious seafood delights.

From succulent prawns to tender calamari and mouthwatering sushi, our menu is a celebration of the ocean's bounty.

Ocean Basket offers a relaxed dining experience that's always filled with flavor and joy.

Dive into a world of seafood perfection at Ocean Basket and create unforgettable moments with every bite.
