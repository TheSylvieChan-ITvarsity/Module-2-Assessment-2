Inception

Director/s: Christopher Nolan

Starring: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt

Age Restriction: PG-13

Synopsis

Inception revolves around Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, a skilled thief who specializes in the art of "extraction" — stealing valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state.

Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform the opposite task of "inception" — planting an idea into someone's mind without them knowing.

To accomplish this seemingly impossible mission, Cobb assembles a team of specialists as they dive deep into layers of shared dreams within dreams.

As they navigate these surreal dreamscapes, facing their own personal demons and challenges, the lines between reality and dream blur. "Inception" is a mind-bending, visually stunning film that explores themes of memory, reality, and the power of the mind.
