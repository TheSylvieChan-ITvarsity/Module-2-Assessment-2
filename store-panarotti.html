Panarottis

Welcome to Panarotti's, where the love for pizza and pasta meets a taste of Italy in every bite.

With a rich heritage spanning over 40 years, Panarotti's is South Africa's go-to spot for authentic Italian cuisine served with a side of family-friendly hospitality.

Our menu boasts a delicious array of wood-fired pizzas, hearty pastas, and mouthwatering grills, made with the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes.

Come experience the flavors of Italy at Panarotti's and indulge in a slice of la dolce vita.
