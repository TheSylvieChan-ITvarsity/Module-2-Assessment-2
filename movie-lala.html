La La Land

Director/s: Damien Chazelle

Starring: Ryan Gosling, Emma Stone

Age Restriction: PG-13

Synopsis

La La Land is a musical romantic comedy-drama film directed by Damien Chazelle.

The story follows Mia, an aspiring actress, and Sebastian, a jazz pianist, as they meet and fall in love in Los Angeles. Their relationship faces challenges as they pursue their dreams and navigate the ups and downs of the entertainment industry.

Set against the backdrop of colorful musical numbers and stunning choreography, "La La Land" is a heartfelt ode to the pursuit of love and artistic ambition, featuring captivating performances by Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling.
